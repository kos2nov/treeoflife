About Natalia:

Natalia graduated from Bellevue Massage School in Washington, earning massage license shortly after. She started her career as a massage practitioner at chiropractor office in Bellevue, WA.

Natalia offers a wide selection of modalities of massage therapy: Swedish, Relaxing, Deep Tissue, Trigger Point release, Myofascial Release, Baby massage.

She is continually amazed of the body’s response to touch. She uses a multiple massage techniques to facilitate a client’s restoration, helping relieve chronic pain and stiffness, relaxing a stressed nervous system.

She has an affinity for helping others and living a healthy life. Natalia found that the power of touch from massage is incredible way to aid others towards better health, and decided to follow this path. With focused touch and energy a person’s body will begin the process of healing.

She believes that patients deserve expert level, as well as a comfortable relationship with their massage therapist mutual trust and respect. Her passion for massage improving patient’s lives enables her to approach each day with the enthusiasm and drive to deliver the utmost quality care. She is thrilled to use this knowledge to best serve her patients.

Natalia earned a Certified Massage Therapist ("CMT") license from California Massage Therapy Council (CMTC). She is also a member of American Massage Therapy Association(AMTA).
